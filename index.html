About Us

The Rhode Island Kung Fu Club is a non-profit martial/cultural arts group practicing kung fu & lion dance in the Providence-area. The RIKFC has taught the martial and cultural arts of Kung Fu and Chinese Lion Dance to Providence-area youth since 2007.
